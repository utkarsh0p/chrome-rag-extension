# Privacy Policy — SiteWhisper

**Last updated:** June 22, 2026

## What data does SiteWhisper collect?

SiteWhisper reads the visible text content of the webpage you are currently viewing only when you actively ask a question through the extension popup. This data is not collected in the background or without your action.

## How is the data used?

The page text is sent to our backend server solely to generate an AI-powered answer to your question. It is not stored, logged, or retained after the response is returned.

## API keys

Your API keys (for Claude, Gemini, OpenAI, or HuggingFace) are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. They are never sent to our servers — they are passed directly to the respective AI provider's API.

## Third-party services

When you ask a question, the page content and your query are sent to the AI provider you selected (Anthropic, Google, OpenAI, or HuggingFace) via their official APIs. Each provider's own privacy policy applies to that interaction.

## Data sharing

SiteWhisper does not sell, share, or transfer user data to any third party for advertising, analytics, or any purpose unrelated to the extension's core functionality.
